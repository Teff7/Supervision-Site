My approach

Relationships at the centre of practice.

Supervision is a collaborative, relational space. The focus is on safety, reflection, and growth, so that you can show up well for the people and communities you work alongside.

Sessions are structured but flexible. There is room for critical thinking, cultural responsiveness, and practical next steps you can take back into your work.

Together we shape a supervision plan that aligns with your role, responsibilities, and professional standards.
